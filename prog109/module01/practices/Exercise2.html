Salad

Created by Isaac Lewis

1/4/2023

Here's a recipe to make my signature salad.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of lettuce
  • tomato

Steps:

  1. Cut the lettuce head until sufficient size has been reached
  2. Slice or medium dice the tomato
  3. Combine in bowl and toss
  4. Serve and enjoy!
