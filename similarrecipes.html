Here is a simple salad recipe that combines lettuce and tomatoes in a bowl:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (such as red wine vinegar or apple cider vinegar)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce and cherry tomatoes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the lettuce and tomatoes and toss to coat.
  4. Serve the salad immediately. Enjoy!
